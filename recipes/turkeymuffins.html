Turkey Muffins

This is a high protein, low calorie recipe by Ethan Chebowski (Youtube).

It's delicious and I usually have 2 muffins for breakfast to start the day!

Ingredients

  • Turkey Mince
  • Cheese
  • Mustard
  • Wholemeal Muffins
  • Spices
  • Maple Syrup

Steps

  1. Combine Turkey mince, spices and maple syrup
  2. Mould the flavoured mince into 4 equal sized balls with your hands
  3. To cook, place each ball in a fry pan on medium-high heat and press hard down to turn into a pattie. Cook 4 mins either side and allow to rest for 2 mins. While resting, place the cheese on top of the pattie to allow the cheese to melt
  4. While waiting for your muffin to cool, toast your wholemeal muffins
  5. Once your muffins are ready, spread the muffin with mustard, add the patty with cheese on top
  6. One muffin will make a delicious breakfast, but if you're looking for lots of energy, feel free to have 2 :)
